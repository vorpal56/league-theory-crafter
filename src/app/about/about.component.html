When you want to change a Champion, their ability level allocations, your rune selection, and many other important factors in League of Legends, you'll need to open the Practice Tool for every separate configuration. That's a lot of configurations!

League Theory Crafter is a quick and easy way to look at a Champion's approximated damage output based on the many different factors in League of Legends without the long switch times when using the Practice Tool. It provides other meaningful calculations, such as the damage distribution, which is important to assess how to deal the most with your unique builds.
Don't fret! Calculations are not meant to be exact values of what's in-game. There are thousands of combinations of interactions that a champion can undergo, so calculations are an approximation of what a champion can do given the circumstances.

If there are large discrepencies in the way damage is calculated, fixes will be dealt with as soon as they are noticed. Contact me so we can get it fixed!
To provide an easy-to use interface, there has to be trade-offs in the way sections are designed. When you increase flexibility, complexity increases and intuitiveness tends to decrease.

Flexibility and customizability is something that can always be improved on and I'm open to suggestions from you guys to make this a better product for the League of Legends community! Send me your suggestions by contacting me and we can work on making those a great addition to the platform.
